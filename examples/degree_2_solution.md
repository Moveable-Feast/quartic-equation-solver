

---
Solve:
$$
x^{2}-3x+2 = 0
$$
Coefficients:
$$
\begin{cases}
    a = 1 \\
    b = -3 \\
    c = 2
\end{cases}
$$
Parameters:
$$
\Delta = b^2 - 4ac = 1
$$
Solutions:
$$
\begin{cases}
x_1 = \dfrac{-b + \sqrt{\Delta}}{2a} = 2 \\
x_2 = \dfrac{-b - \sqrt{\Delta}}{2a} = 1
\end{cases}
$$

Solve:
$$
(1+i)x^{2}+(-3-i)x+(2+i) = 0
$$
Coefficients:
$$
\begin{cases}
    a = 1+i \\
    b = -3-i \\
    c = 2+i
\end{cases}
$$
Parameters:
$$
\Delta = b^2 - 4ac = 4-6i
$$
Solutions:
$$
\begin{cases}
x_1 = \dfrac{-b + \sqrt{\Delta}}{2a} = 1.2751-1.4087i \\
x_2 = \dfrac{-b - \sqrt{\Delta}}{2a} = 0.7249+0.4087i
\end{cases}
$$

where
$$
i = \sqrt{-1}.
$$
